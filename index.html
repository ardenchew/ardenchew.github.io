ABOUT ME

Hi! My Name's Arden.

 

In the spring of 2014 I blew out my right knee and spent months resigned to a couch. As an avid athlete and self-proclaimed adrenaline junkie, I wanted nothing more than to get off that couch. I gained a newfound appreciation for people such as my Grandmother, who continues to travel the world with the help of her prosthetic leg. Since recovering from my injury, I have committed myself to applying my talents as a developer and engineer to ensure others can share in the human right to mobility and health.
